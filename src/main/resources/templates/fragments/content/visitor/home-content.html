What is ApplicaTrack?

The smarter way to manage your entire hiring pipeline.

ApplicaTrack is a web-based organizational system designed to empower HR teams and personnel to manage applicants efficiently. It tracks every candidate from the moment they submit their application, through resume reviewing, interview scheduling, and all the way to employment — keeping every step organized, visible, and fair.

Our built-in AI ensures every resume is reviewed objectively against the job description — so the most qualified candidates rise to the top, every single time.

The Process

From Application to Employment — Every Step Tracked.

A structured, transparent pipeline that keeps HR in control and candidates informed.

1

Application Submission

Candidates fill out their profile and upload their resume through a guided form. All data is instantly captured and organized.

2

AI Resume Review

Our AI scores every resume against the job description — evaluating qualifications fairly and consistently across all applicants.

Real-time AI scoring
3

Interview Scheduling

Shortlisted candidates receive an automated interview invitation. HR can confirm, reschedule, or cancel from the dashboard.

4

Employment Decision

HR finalizes decisions. Qualified candidates move to onboarding; others receive a professional update — everyone stays in the loop.
